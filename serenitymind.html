SerenityMind

SerenityMind is an AI-powered mental wellness application designed to support emotional well-being through private, offline-first AI interactions. The system provides mood-aware conversations, encrypted journaling, emotional tracking, and self-care guidance using locally hosted language models to ensure privacy and low latency.

Tech Stack

Frontend and backend: Streamlit AI Models: LLaMA 3, Mistral, Phi AI Runtime: Ollama (Local Inference) Data Storage: Local Encrypted Files Security: AES-256 Encryption

Implementation Screens

Key Features

  • Mood-aware conversational AI with empathetic responses
  • Fully offline AI inference for enhanced privacy
  • Encrypted journaling with secure local storage
  • Mood tracking and emotional trend visualization
  • Age-adaptive and mode-based response personalization

Upcoming Features
